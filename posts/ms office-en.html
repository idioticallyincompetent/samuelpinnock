Software suite

MS Office

MS Office is the most widely used office productivity suite, covering the core tasks of everyday work: word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, and email communication.

Word

Word is used for writing and formatting documents — from quick notes to lengthy reports with tables of contents, style templates, and tracked changes.

Excel

Excel handles managing, calculating, and visualizing tabular data — from simple lists to complex formulas, pivot tables, and charts.

PowerPoint & Outlook

PowerPoint is used for building presentations, and Outlook for email and scheduling — both are a fixed part of daily work at nearly every company.
